Gender

“ A word which indicates male or female sex is called Gender . ”

Types Of Gender :-

    • Masculline Gender

      • A word which indicates male sex is called Masculline Gender.

      Ex. Horse, Dog, Son etc.

    • Feminine Gender

      • A word which indicates female sex is called Feminine Gender.

      Ex. Mare, Bitch, Daughter etc.

    • Common Gender

      • Male or Female category of person or animal is called Common Gender.

      Note :

      1. There is no boundation.
      2. Pronoun → He & His { If subject is not clear then prefer masculline gender. }

    • Neuter Gener

      • Non-Living objects and lower animals are called Neuter Gender .

      Ex. Pen, Pencil, Snake, Child, Insects, Germs, Infacts etc.

      Note :

      Use Pronoun :- It

      • Third Person : Male + Female = One person is called Third Person.
      • Bisexual : Male + Female = One animal.
      • Homosexual ⁄ Homophsodite : Male + Female = One plant.
